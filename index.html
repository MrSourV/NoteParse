Frequently asked questions

What does "unlimited pages" mean?
Free users can convert up to 20 images per session. Pro users can upload and convert as many as they want.
How does the AI chat work?
After converting your notes, Claude has full context of everything. You can ask it to summarise, explain, quiz you, or answer any question. Free users get 5 messages; Pro gets unlimited.
What are flashcards?
After conversion, click "Flashcards" to auto-generate question/answer cards from your notes. Perfect for studying.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — cancel with one click, no questions asked. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.

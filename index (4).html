Free users can convert up to 20 images per session. Pro users can upload and convert as many as they want in a single conversion.

After converting your notes, Claude has full context of everything in your document. You can ask it to summarise, explain, quiz you, or answer any question about the content. Free users get 5 messages per session; Pro gets unlimited.
